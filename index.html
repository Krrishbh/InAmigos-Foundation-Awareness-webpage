About Us

A collective force driving community transformation since 2020.

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO dedicated to creating positive social change. Through impactful initiatives in education, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare and community development, the organization strives to build a better future for all.
